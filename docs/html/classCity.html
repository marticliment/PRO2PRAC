Detailed Description

Represents a city with an inventory of Product items.

The City class stores information about a city, including its unique identifier, inventory of products, weight, and volume. It provides methods to manage the inventory, such as adding, updating, and removing products, as well as retrieving information about the products and trading with other cities.
